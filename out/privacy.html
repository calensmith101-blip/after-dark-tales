Privacy Policy

After Dark Tales — Last updated: April 2026

We keep data collection to a minimum.

Profile names, profile images, reading counters, and age-verification status are stored on your device.

Stories are generated by AI in response to your request.

When you request a story, the genre, tone, length, and story notes you provide are sent to our AI provider for generation.

After Dark Tales is intended for adults aged 18 and over.

A local age-verification check is used to confirm eligibility on this device.

1. Our Approach to Privacy

After Dark Tales is designed to minimise the information we collect and store. Most app data stays on your device, and we use third-party services only where needed to provide story generation and app hosting.

We do not sell your information, and we do not use your profile details for advertising.

2. Who This Service Is For

After Dark Tales is intended only for adults aged 18 and over. If a user cannot confirm that they are an adult, access should not be granted.

3. Age Verification

On first use, you are asked to confirm that you are 18 or over. This check is used to control access to the app on your device.

A local verification flag may be stored on your device so you do not need to repeat the check each time.

4. AI-Generated Content

All stories in After Dark Tales are generated by AI. Stories are created based on the genre, tone, length, and story notes you provide.

Content safeguards

We use automated safeguards intended to block prohibited requests, including content involving minors, non-consensual sexual content, bestiality, incest, necrophilia, and certain violent or hateful material.

Reporting

If generated content appears to break our content rules, you can use the in-app reporting feature so that we can improve our safeguards.

5. Information Used by the App

Stored on your device

Profile names, profile images, usage counters, credit balances, and age-verification status may be stored locally on your device.

Story requests

When you request a story, the genre, style, length, and any story notes you provide are sent to our AI provider for the purpose of generating content.

Technical service data

Our hosting provider may process standard technical request data, such as IP address, timestamps, and device-related information, for security, reliability, and performance.

6. Third-Party Services

After Dark Tales may rely on third-party providers such as an AI API provider and a hosting platform. Those providers may process data as needed to deliver their services.

Please avoid entering sensitive personal information into story prompts.

7. Children

This service is not intended for children. It should not be used by anyone under 18.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date shown at the top of this page.

9. Plain Language Summary

After Dark Tales stores most app information on your device. Story requests are sent to an AI provider so stories can be generated. A hosting provider may process standard technical logs to keep the service running. The service is intended for adults aged 18 and over.
